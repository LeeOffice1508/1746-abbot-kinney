Property Overview

A Creative Campus
On Iconic Ground

Rarely available on one of Los Angeles' most celebrated corridors, 1746 Abbot Kinney offers a campus-style creative office compound unlike anything else in Venice. With 6,055 SF of highly improved space, the property delivers a converted residential feel with a sophisticated edge — featuring polished concrete, hardwood floors, soaring ceilings, and skylights that flood the interior with natural light.

Seamless indoor-outdoor flow opens to expansive landscaped patio and courtyard areas, perfect for meetings, events, or everyday team culture. Surrounded by the best of Abbot Kinney's dining, retail, and creative community, this is a rare opportunity to anchor your brand in one of LA's most walkable, high-visibility corridors.
